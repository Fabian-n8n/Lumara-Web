Core Pillars

Engineered for Well-Being

Four foundational pillars guide every design decision, ensuring that each Lumara intelligent toilet delivers on the promise of thoughtful, human-centered innovation.

Human-Centered Design

Inspired by organic forms found in nature, each silhouette balances sculptural beauty with ergonomic precision. Every curve serves a purpose.

Advanced Cleanliness

Proprietary UV-light sterilization technology paired with stainless steel nozzles ensures hospital-grade hygiene with every use cycle.

Touch-Free Hygiene

Automatic lid operation, sensor-activated flushing, and proximity-based controls eliminate the need for physical contact entirely.

Deep Personalization

Individual user profiles store preferred water temperature, pressure, seat warmth, and ambient lighting. Your comfort, memorized.

Ambient Intelligence

Integrated night lighting, automatic deodorization, and whisper-quiet operation adapt seamlessly to any time of day.

Lasting Durability

Premium ceramic bodies with antimicrobial glazing resist stains and bacteria, maintaining their pristine finish for decades of use.

Technology

Where Precision Meets Intuition

Our engineering teams combine decades of expertise in fluid dynamics, thermal management, and sensor technology to create systems that respond before you even reach for a button.

01

Adaptive Sensor Array

Multi-point proximity sensors detect approach, seating position, and departure to automate lid, flush, and cleaning cycles.

02

ThermoCore Heating

Rapid-response ceramic heating elements reach target seat and water temperatures in under three seconds, consuming minimal energy.

03

PureStream Wash System

Oscillating and pulsating wash modes with adjustable pressure deliver a personalized, thorough cleansing experience every time.

04

AirPurify Deodorizer

Activated carbon filtration combined with catalytic ionization neutralizes odors at the molecular level, not simply masking them.
